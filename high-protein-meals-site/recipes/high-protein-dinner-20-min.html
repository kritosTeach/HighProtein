20-Min High Protein Teriyaki Salmon Bowl (45g Protein)

⏱️ 20 min | 🍗 45g | 🔥 520 cal
Calories 520
Protein 45g
Carbs 40g
Fat 22g

Ingredients (1 bowl)

  • 6 oz frozen salmon fillet (30g protein)
  • 1 cup microwave brown rice
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 tbsp low-sugar teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds, green onion

Instructions

  1. Microwave rice 90 sec, broccoli 3 min. Set aside.
  2. Air fryer or bake salmon 400°F 12 min (or microwave defrost + pan 5 min).
  3. Mix teriyaki + sesame oil, brush salmon. Broil 1 min.
  4. Assemble bowl: rice base, broccoli, salmon. Toppings.
