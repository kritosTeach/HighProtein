10-Min High Protein Breakfast Burrito (35g Protein)

⏱️ Prep: 10 min | Cook: 5 min 🍗 35g Protein | 🔥 450 Calories Servings: 1 | Macros: 30C/20F/35P
Calories
450
Protein
35g
Carbs
30g
Fat
20g
Fiber
6g

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs (12g protein)
  • 2 oz pre-cooked turkey sausage (15g protein)
  • 1 large whole wheat tortilla (8")
  • ¼ cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese (7g protein)
  • ¼ avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp salsa
  • Salt, pepper, hot sauce to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat skillet over medium. Add turkey sausage, cook 2-3 min until browned. Crumble.
  2. Push sausage aside, crack eggs into pan. Scramble with sausage 2 min until set.
  3. Warm tortilla in microwave 15 sec or dry skillet.
  4. Spoon egg mixture onto tortilla center. Top with cheese, avocado slices, salsa.
  5. Fold bottom up, roll sides in, roll tightly. Optional: Pan-sear 30 sec per side for crisp.
  6. Serve immediately with hot sauce. Done in 10 minutes!

FAQ

Can I make this egg-free?
Yes, sub firm tofu scrambled with turmeric (22g protein).
Is this keto-friendly?
Use low-carb tortilla: 5g net carbs total.
Can I meal prep?
Make 3 burritos, wrap individually, fridge 3 days.
