15-Min High Protein Chicken Salad Wrap (40g Protein)

Calories
380
Protein
40g
Carbs
25g
Fat
12g

Ingredients (1 large wrap)

  • 8 oz rotisserie chicken breast, shredded (35g protein)
  • ¼ cup plain 0% Greek yogurt (6g protein)
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • ¼ cup red grapes, halved
  • 1 tbsp chopped pecans (optional)
  • Low carb tortilla (8-10")
  • Lettuce leaf, mustard, salt/pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix chicken, yogurt, celery, grapes, pecans. Season.
  2. Lay tortilla flat, add lettuce leaf.
  3. Spoon chicken salad (2 cups), mustard. Roll tightly.
  4. Slice diagonal, serve. Ready in 15 min!
